Home Alone

If there won’t be anyone home when you’re traveling for the holidays, here are some tips to protect your house while you’re away

 • Don’t post about your travels on social media until after you get home

 • Keep up regular maintenance - Have someone grab your mail or put it on hold, and have your driveway and sidewalk cleared of snow so it looks like someone is there each day

 • Put indoor and outdoor lights on timers

 • Remove your hidden keys outside, but give a key to a trusted neighbor in the case that they need to check on something inside

 • Hide Christmas gifts and packages away from porches and windows

Peanut Butter Fudge

Ingredients 

 • 2 cups of sugar

 • 7 oz marshmallow fluff

 • 12 oz creamy or crunchy peanut butter

 • 1/2 cup whole milk

Instructions

 • Butter a small pyrex or 8×8 baking dish and set aside.

 • In a medium saucepan (preferably non-stick), combine the milk and sugar, and bring to a boil. Continue to boil, stirring constantly, for three minutes.

 • Remove the pan from the heat and add the peanut butter and marshmallow fluff. Stir until well combined.

 • Spread the fudge into the baking dish. Allow to cool to room temperature, and then refrigerate.

Welcome to the Barrett Insurance family, Tiffany Jagger!

Hey, I’m Tiffany. I was born here in Michigan and raised on the Georgia side of Chattanooga, Tennessee. With both of my parents being teachers, I spent many holidays and most summers here in Hillsdale County, where my family has resided since the 1880’s.  

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Education from Valdosta State University in 2014. As a 5th generation teacher, I love learning, keeping up to date on new developments, and spending time educating people.

After managing a doctor’s office and experiencing health insurance from a provider’s perspective, I am now on the preventive side of insurance, working with clients to help them find their best option. My focus is on wellness and maintaining consistent coverage while advocating for preventive care. Get your yearly physical, folks!

When I am out of the office, I enjoy searching for interesting finds at thrift stores with my family, weekend road trips, healthy junk food, writing sentences that are too long, and learning to fly.
