To Our Valued Customers

Please know that our thoughts and prayers go out to all those impacted by this Coronavirus.  

In coordination with Governor Whitmer’s Executive Order, we are limiting our office staff to just a couple agents, with everyone else working remotely. We’re still open as usual, we’re just set up a little differently now. Please know that as the COVID-19 pandemic brings us into uncharted territory, we are committed to doing our part to minimize the spread of the virus while also providing responsive service to our clients.

For the safety of our clients and staff, our office is open by appointment only. We will limit the number of appointments in the office for essential services only. We are available by phone at 517-849-1000 and email. You can find a list of specific agent email addresses here.

Payments can be made by calling your insurance carrier by phone or mailing it to the company. If you require additional assistance please call our office 517-849-1000.

Thank you for your patience and understanding during this time.

Our commitment to you, your family, and your business has not changed. We are here to serve!
